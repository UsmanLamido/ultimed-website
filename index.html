Maternity & Women's Health

Complete obstetric care.
From booking to discharge.

ULTI-MED's Maternity Suite is purpose-built for Nigerian obstetric practice — covering the full continuum with clinical safety tools that directly address Nigeria's maternal mortality crisis.

🤱

Why Maternity Needs Its Own Module

Nigeria has one of the highest maternal mortality rates in the world. Eclampsia mismanagement, delayed recognition of obstructed labour, and incomplete clinical documentation are leading contributors — and all are directly addressable through structured, intelligent software. ULTI-MED Maternity was built for that responsibility.

🤰

Antenatal Clinic (ANC)

Complete ANC capture per visit: LMP, EDD, gravida/para history, fundal height, foetal presentation, blood group, genotype, BP, TT immunisation, iron supplementation, and WHO protocol compliance. NHIS capitation auto-generated per visit.

All Editions
📉

Digital Labour Care Guide

Replaces the paper partograph with an intelligent digital guide. Real-time cervical dilation, foetal heart rate, contractions, maternal vitals, and descent — with automated threshold alerts for prolonged or obstructed labour.

All Editions
⚠️

Eclampsia Protocol

Automatic alert when BP crosses pre-eclampsia threshold. Structured management protocol prompts immediately. Every intervention documented with timestamp. Full audit trail from first alert to delivery outcome.

Critical Safety
💊

MgSO₄ Dosing Calculator

Integrated Magnesium Sulphate protocol — loading dose and maintenance dose calculated by patient weight. Toxicity monitoring alerts for patellar reflexes, urine output, and respiratory depression. Eliminates the most dangerous drug calculation in obstetrics.

Critical Safety
👶

Delivery & Birth Records

Complete intrapartum documentation — mode of delivery, duration of labour stages, APGAR scores, birth weight, newborn resuscitation details, cord blood, placenta details, episiotomy, blood loss, and maternal post-delivery status.

All Editions
🍼

Postnatal & Newborn Care

Mother and baby tracked together from delivery through discharge. BCG/HBV immunisation, jaundice monitoring, breastfeeding support, postnatal visit scheduling, newborn examination, and family planning counselling documentation.

All Editions
🔬

High-Risk Pregnancy Tracking

Auto-flags hypertension, gestational diabetes, multiple gestation, previous CS, grand multiparity, anaemia, sickle cell, and HIV — with tailored care pathways and escalation protocols per risk category.

Specialist & Prime
📊

Maternal Health Statistics

Generates MMR, stillbirth rate, CS rate, APGAR distribution, low birth weight rate — all ICD-11 coded and formatted for HMIS submission, MDCN reporting, and publication-ready research outputs.
