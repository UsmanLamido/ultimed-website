Dangerous Drug Interactions Going Undetected

Without automated interaction checks, your clinical staff are prescribing blindly. One medical error can cost far more than your annual IT budget — in litigation, reputation, and lives.

Risk: Litigation + Loss of Licence
🚪

Services Rendered Without Payment

Without a Service Gate, your hospital delivers procedures, labs, and medications that never get billed. Staff bypass the system, patients walk out, revenue disappears.

Avg. Loss: ₦1.1M/year per 100 beds
📄

NHIS Claims Rejected at Alarming Rates

Coding errors, wrong ICD versions, missing documentation — HMOs reject claims on technicalities. Most hospitals lose 20–40% of their capitation value through preventable errors.

Avg. Loss: ₦800K/year in rejected claims
📊

Incomplete Data Killing Research & Audits

When staff skip fields, enter inconsistent data, or use free-text for structured information, your records become legally indefensible and statistically useless.

Risk: Failed Accreditation Audits
🤝

HMO & Retainer Agreement Confusion

Private HMOs, NHIS, State Health Schemes, Retainerships — each with different rules. Without a policy engine, your staff apply the wrong tariff daily, creating disputes and write-offs.

Avg. Loss: ₦600K/year in write-offs
